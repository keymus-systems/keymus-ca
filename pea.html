How to Build a Profile That Gets Noticed

Follow these steps to maximise your visibility with Canadian employers

01
Add a Professional Photo

Profiles with a clear, professional headshot receive significantly more employer views. Use a neutral background and dress as you would for an interview.

02
Write a Strong Headline

Your headline appears in employer search results. Be specific: "Registered Nurse — 7 yrs ICU experience, open to BC/AB" outperforms a generic "Healthcare Professional".

03
Upload Your Latest Resume

Always keep your resume current. Use a Canadian resume format — 1 to 2 pages, no photo on the document itself, and tailored to your target role.

04
List Your Top Skills

Employers filter searches by skill keywords. Add at least 10 relevant skills to your profile — hard skills (Python, CPR, AutoCAD) and soft skills (leadership, communication).
