Who We Are

About Keymus Jobs

A Canadian-based jobs platform built for the modern workforce — newcomers, remote workers, and ambitious professionals alike.

Connecting Canadians to Work Since 2005

Keymus started in 2005 helping companies manage logistics and staffing more efficiently. As the Canadian workforce evolved, so did we — expanding into digital recruitment in 2012 and launching our full-service online jobs platform in 2018.

Canada's Trusted Job Platform

Today, Keymus partners with thousands of Canadian employers and independent workers to match skills with real, paying roles. Headquartered in Canada, we serve job seekers across every province and support newcomers navigating the Canadian job market for the first time. We're just getting started.
