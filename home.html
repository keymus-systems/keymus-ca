What Is a PEA?

A Product Engagement Associate (PEA) is an independent contributor who helps new and emerging products gain traction on major e-commerce platforms. PEAs participate in structured campaigns that drive product visibility, engagement, and momentum during critical early launch phases.

Who Are PEAs?

PEAs are independent freelancers who appreciate flexible work, performance-based pay, and the chance to be part of fast-moving product campaigns. No experience is needed—just consistency, reliability, and a willingness to complete a simple onboarding process.

Are PEAs Part of Keymus?

Absolutely. Though independent freelancers, PEAs are a valued part of the Keymus family. Working side by side on store campaigns, they help grow revenue while earning well for themselves. With Keymus’s full support and resources, PEAs have everything they need to thrive and succeed.
