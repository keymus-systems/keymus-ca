Quick Tips for Your Canadian Job Search

Tailor Every Resume

Generic applications rarely succeed. Match your keywords to the job description and emphasise the skills the employer explicitly asks for.

Optimise Your LinkedIn Profile

Most Canadian recruiters source candidates on LinkedIn. Use a professional photo, write a strong headline, and ask for recommendations from past colleagues.

Network Strategically

Up to 70% of Canadian jobs are filled through connections before being publicly advertised. Attend industry events, join professional associations, and connect online.

Get Your Credentials Recognised

If you trained outside Canada, check whether your credentials need to be assessed. Many regulated professions require Canadian equivalency before you can work.

Prepare for Behavioural Interviews

Canadian employers frequently use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result). Practice answering questions with specific examples from your experience.
